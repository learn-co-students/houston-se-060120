### HTTP Form-Behavior Method Table Thingy: 
### HTTP Methods

#### GET

- #### Default

- #### Cannot have a body

- #### Supported by Form tag directly

#### POST

- #### Can have a body

- #### Supported by Form tag directly

#### PATCH

- #### Can have a body

- #### Not Supported by Form tag directly

#### PUT

- #### Can have a body

- #### Not Supported by Form tag directly

#### DELETE

- #### Cannot have a body

- #### Not Supported by Form tag directly
